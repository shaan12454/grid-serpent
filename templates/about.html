About This Game

Welcome to our multiplayer Snake-style game! Here, you can challenge yourself in several exciting modes:

  • Classic Mode: Enjoy the traditional snake experience and grow as long as you can without hitting the walls.
  • Extreme Mode: Navigate a massive, full-screen map filled with walls and obstacles, making the game extra challenging.
  • Speed Run: Test your reflexes as the snake moves faster and faster. Can you survive?

Every score you achieve is automatically recorded, and you can compare your progress with other players on the leaderboard
